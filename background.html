Uzma Ferdous is a freelance artist, entrepeneur, and student.

Born on March 26, 2003, in Welland, Ontario, the boredom of her surroundings allowed her to develop an overactive imagination and social ineptitude, among other skills. Her artistry grabs inspiration from Pinterest posts and random shower thoughts recorded on her Notes app.

Besides painting, Uzma spends her time programming, weightlifting, and making up names for her paintings as she creates this website. She takes commissions.
