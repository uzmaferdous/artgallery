The S.h.o.t.

This painting serves as an homage to Uzma Ferdous' favourite athlete, Kawhi Leonard, as well as her AUX cord go-to, Kendrick Lamar. She draws inspiration from the album cover of 'good kid, m.A.A.d city'.

Marilyn x Medusa

The greatest Hollywood starlette the world has ever seen...and the most villainized Greek maiden. Both symbols of feminine power, and supremely misunderstood. This painting seeks to embrace their common ground.

Intergalactic

As Ferdous' first painting, it was originally meant to be practice in order to get acquainted with her new supplies. As she was always enamoured with outer space and the night sky, Ferdous simply let her imagination run wild in this piece.

Tame Impala Cover

A simple take on Tame Impala's album cover for 'Currents'. The psychadelic music on this album helped Ferdous get through the troubling, treacherous high school years.

Nazar Forest

A surreal, simplistic forest that Ferdous claims to have gotten lost within in one of her dreams. With lurking eyes and scarily mishapen plants, the Nazar Forest seems to house some divine spirits.

RE-INVENT

This painting references one of Uzma's prized childhood toys, the Rubix cube. As someone who has dealt with an irritating level of perfectionism, Uzma believes conquering this involves re-inventing the set standards she desperately yearns for.
